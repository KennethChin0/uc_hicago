    How to Play

    Goal of the Game

    • The player will face off against a bot. (Each team will have 6 Pokemon)
    • The purpose is to defeat the enemy's Pokemon before they can defeat you.

    Setting Up

    • Players can choose their team in the teambuilder tab.
    • Players will be able to store multiple teams.
    • Players will have the option to choose any Pokemon up to generation 5.
    • You can choose the 4 moves for each of the Pokemon to be used in battle in battle. (moves are restricted to certain Pokemon)
    • Players are given points to increase stats.

    Battle

    • The game is turn-based.
    • Players are given the option to attack or switch out Pokemon.
    • The bot will attack or switch out Pokemon each time the player performs an action.

    Mechanics

    • Each Pokemon has its own type which makes them invulnerable/weaker/stronger against certain types
